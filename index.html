About Me

My name is Quentin Datcher and I'm a 24 year old DC native raised in SE DC. As a youth I attended Kimball Elementry and Sousa Middleschool playing baseball and joining the school band/drumline, thus sparking my love for music. During my highschool years at School Without Walls I joined the crew team and worked for the National Air and Space museum as an explainer.

In college, I pursued an electrical engineering degree at the University of Pitsburgh @ Johnstown, however a lack of finances would later force me to drop out in my junior year.

Looking for a better source of income, I joined a sales company that was looking to expand to Texas selling cell service to business owners in the Houston area. Through the process I picked up a lot of soft skills and met a lot of great friends however I knew the world of sales wasn't a longterm option for me so when the pandemic hit, I moved back to DC and was lucky to get recommended to my current job at Congressional Cemetery.

What I do for fun?

  • Music - I primarily play guitar/uke, however in recent years I've been learning bass and keyboard. Every once in a while I'll post on on my youtube channel
  • Kickboxing/MMA - For the past year and a half I've been learning how to kicbox and more recently have started jiujitsu and some catch wrestling (My skill level is still very beginner as shown in my sparring video)
  • Going outside- Everyone needs fresh air so I go around on hikes or adventuring to museums, festivals, and other events.
  • Culture Consumption - Anime/Manga, videogames,
